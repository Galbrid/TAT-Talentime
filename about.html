About Talentime Academy

Premier medical coding training with examination and career support.

Founded in 2021, Talentime Academy of Medical Coding provides specialized training for students and working professionals seeking certification in medical coding. Our curriculum is built around the CPC certification standards, real-world clinical case studies, and practical skills for healthcare revenue cycle management.

Our mission

To equip learners with the knowledge, confidence, and exam readiness needed to succeed as certified medical coders.

Why choose us

We focus on live support, structured practice, and certification alignment so students can clear exams and secure coding roles faster.

Our promise

Clear guidance, updated course materials, and dedicated instructor help across the complete certification journey.
